Known Issues Summary (from testing above)

  • Abbreviations ending with punctuation (.,:;) - May wrap only first character
  • Quote at paragraph start - No space before, may not convert to opening quote
  • Ligatures in URLs - Will incorrectly create ligatures in technical URLs
  • Numbers in decimals - Each part wrapped separately (3.14 becomes <num>3</num>.<num>14</num>)
  • Contractions at start - 'twas, 'til may not convert properly without leading space
  • Multiple consecutive quotes - May produce unexpected results
  • Abbreviations with periods - U.S.A. gets split incorrectly
  • Two dots - Not converted to ellipsis (only 3+ dots)
